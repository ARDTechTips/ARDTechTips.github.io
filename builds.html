Welcome to the How To Build page

This is where you can learn how to build a PC

Step one: Getting the Components

The essential part of computer is the parts. These will include a motherboard, CPU, RAM, HDD or SSD, Cooling and a case. A dedicated GPU is optional but if you want the best gaming, rendering or crypto currency mining experience it is recommended as even though CPU's have an onboard GPU it isn't golf enough to run games at a high frame rate.

Step two: inserting the motherboard

Inserting the motherboard should be easy if your case has space on the inside. It is as easy as follows, get the mother board out of the box, insert it into the case, then if all the holes ON THE MOTHERBOARD fit the holes on the case, then just use the included screws and screw to in.

Step three: Inserting the CPU

Inserting the CPU is easy, all you have to do is align the connectors on the CPU and on the motherboard and just drop it on, FROM A TINY HEIGHT, DO NOT THROW, PUSH OR DROP FROM DISTANCE, then just put the cover back on the CPU and insert the clip under the cover keeping it closed

Step four: Inserting RAM

Inserting the RAM is the easiest thing in this hole process, it is just placing the ram HORIZONTALLY into the RAM slot and pressing until you hear it click into place, make sure to open the slot first.

Step Five: Inserting GPU (Optional)

inserting the GPU is quite hard. Fist of all remove the bracket on the case and then removing the amount of GPU input brackets required, then slide the GPU into the GPU slot, VERTICALLY AND NOT AT SLANT AND NOT TO HARD SO NOT TO DAMAGE THE GPU CONNECTERS, once done screw the GPU input bracket to where the old brackets went Andrew then re-screw the original bracket.

Step six: inserting the SSD or HDD

This is quite a care free task, all you need to do, get the SSD or HDD and take one of the mounting brackets out of the SSD or HDD bay and screw the SSD or HDD to the mounting bracket and then take the SATA cable and connect it to the SDD or HDD, then put it back in the SSD or HDD bay.

Step seven: Cooling

Cooling is simple, take your fans and screw them in to desired places. IF USING CUSTOM WATER COOLING PLEASE VISIT THIS
